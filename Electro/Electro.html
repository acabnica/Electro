Electro

Electro is one of the seven Elements and the third element acquired by the Traveler. Its associated Archon is Ei, whose domain is Inazuma.

What is Electro?

An electro vision allows its wielder to battle with the electro element (electricity). Holders with electro vision are either outcasts or oddities in their respective society. They are unable to accept the world as it is. Electro Visions empowers individuals who have been beaten by the world and feel as if they have no place to call home other than themselves. Because of their determination to remain where they are, Baal may have given them visions to reward their perseverance. Most Electro users have experienced trauma or some type of adversity, and there have been times in their lives when they felt as if they were struck by lightning. Unpredictable, intense, and capable of turning their lives upside down in an instant.

Electro Status

Electro has no unique impact on its own, but it allows Electro Elemental Reactions to occur. In Inazuma, some mechanisms, such as the Lightning Strike Probe, will react to characters or monsters impacted by Electro. The Electro status will be applied by any source of Electro DMG, including Electro Crystals. During a thunderstorm, being struck by lightning or entering a body of water struck by lightning will also apply Electro.

Electro Charcters

Electro Characters

As of now there are 9 Electro Character. They are Beidou, Fischl, Keqing, Kojou Sara, Kuki Shinobu, Lisa, Raiden Shogun, Razor, and Yae Miko.

Beidou

Beidou, the fearless captain of the Crux. Aside from her talents as a ship commander and her tremendous strength, many in Liyue know her for her lack of fear of the Tianquan of the Liyue Qixing, Ningguang – a trait that the other admires yet finds irritating.

Fischl von Luftschloss Narfidort

Fischl, the Prinzessin der Verurteilung. Fischl, followed by the night raven Oz, is an investigator for Mondstadt's Adventurers' Guild. Fischl claims to come from a world other than Teyvat. She is a brave explorer with seemingly absurd beliefs that turn out to be correct.

Keqing

As the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, she pursues her own solutions rather than passively allowing chaos to reign in Liyue. Instead than allowing the gods to choose her fate, she chooses her own route with her own strength and talent.

Kujou Sara

Sara, a tengu, is the adoptive daughter of the Tenryou Commission's Kujou Clan. Sara, who is loyal to both her clan and the Shogun, follows the Shogun's demands.

Kuki Shinobu

Kuki Ninja, is an upcoming Electro character in Genshin Impact. She is the sidekick of Arataki Itto and Deputy Leader of the Arataki Gang.

Lisa Minci

Lisa, a strong wizard who was named Sumeru Academia's most renowned graduate in 200 years, has subsequently returned to Mondstadt to become the Knights of Favonius' humble Librarian. She may be gotten for free through the mission Sparks Amongst the Pages in the Prologue Act I: The Outlander Who Caught the Wind.

Raiden Shogun

Raiden Shogun is a fierce lady in the Genshin ranks, dishing off powerful electrical assaults as well as a variety of utility. She is the oppressive ruler of Inazuma who is worshiped by her people. She is also known as Baal and the God of Eternity.

Razor

Razor was abandoned as a newborn and raised by the Wolf of the North, Andrius, and his wolf pack in Wolvendom. He has slowly begun to integrate with humans again after an accidental encounter with Varka, the current Grand Master of the Knights of Favonius and the Knights of Boreas.

Yae Miko

Yae Miko, also known as Guuji Yae; the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine and owner of Yae Publishing House, Genshin Impact’s Yae Miko‘s elegant and beautiful appearance belies an intelligent and cunning personality fitting of her kitsune heritage
